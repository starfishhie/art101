lab 12 - conditionals

challenge

the challenge for this lab was to use what we learned about conditionals or if/else statements to make a function and button that would sort the user into a category based on the remainder gotten from the length of their text input when divided by the number of groups.

problems

i didn't run into any major issues or roadblocks with this lab! i just had to occassionally look at the example code in the instructions or reference older labs to remind myself on how to combine my new sorter function with button listeners. just a quick skim did the trick though!

reflection

i didn't really have much trouble with this lab! my functions are pretty simple and straightforward and they do what the lab asks us to so i'm content with them being just functional for now, but in the future i might mess around with them to see how far i can push what the button can do and make it do a something a little more interesting than just displaying a small line of text.
